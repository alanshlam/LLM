🔒 The Privacy Paradox Resolved {#the-privacy-paradox-resolved .text-2xl .font-bold .text-sky-400 .mb-4 .flex .items-center .gap-2}
------------------------------

For years, personal productivity meant surrendering sensitive
data---finances, private emails, and daily schedules---to corporate
cloud servers. The Sovereign Agent flips this paradigm. By leveraging
quantized Large Language Models (LLMs) on local hardware, it delivers
elite executive assistance with absolutely zero data leakage. Your data
never leaves your machine.
